Navigating the Website

  1. Sign into your account with the login button in the top right, can also make an account if necessary
  2. The "Reserve Times" tab allows the user to schedule a time to play the games uninterrupted
  3. The "Games" tab displays your favorited games and new ones to try
  4. The "Cost" tab displays the cost for each of the games
